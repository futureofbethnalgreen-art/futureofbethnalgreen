Object to the 520-bed student block proposed for the landmark LEB Building, opposite Bethnal Green Gardens.

Application Ref · PA/26/00212/A1 255–279 Cambridge Heath Rd · E2 0EL Send before the deadline

Tower Hamlets is one of the most deprived communities in the UK — and yet the proposed development offers it nothing. No social homes. No public space. No local jobs. No cultural contribution. Just 520 bedsits let to international students at £1,400–£1,600 a month. It is a shameless attempt from a major US owned developer to maximise profit by exploiting students. This is a prime site that could anchor the regeneration of Bethnal Green.

Your councillors represent you. Below is a ready-to-send objection. Add your name and address, personalise it if you wish, and send it in two steps.
